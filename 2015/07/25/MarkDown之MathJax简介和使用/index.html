MathJax basic tutorial and quick reference

  1. 行内公式和段落公式

    For inline formulas, enclose the formula in $...$. For displayed formulas, use $$...$$.
    These render differently: $\sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}$ (inline)or

    $$\sum_{i=0}^n i^{2} = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}\tag{display}$$

    the tex command:$$\sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}\tag{display}$$

  2. 希腊字符

  1. 上标和下标

    For superscipts and subscripts,use ^ and _.For example,x_i^2:$x_i^{2}$

  2. 组.

    Superscripts, subscripts, and other operations apply only to the next “group”. A “group” is either a single symbol, or any formula surrounded by curly braces {...}.If you do 10^10,you will get a suprise:$10^10$.But 10^{10} give what you probably wanted:$10^{10}$.Use curly bracess to delimit a formula to which a superscripts or subscript applies:x^5^6is an error;{x^y}^z is ${x^y}^z$.Observe the difference between x_i^2$xi^2$ and `x{i^2}$x_{i^2}$ 补充：如果要在左右两边都有上下标，可以\sideset`命令

    \sideset{^1_2}{^3_4}\bigotimes $\sideset{^1_2}{^3_4}\bigotimes$

  3. 各种括号

    Oridinary symbol()[]make parentheses and brackets (2+3)[4+4].Use \{ and \}for curly braces.

    These do not scale with the formula in between,so if you write(\frac{\sqrt x}{y^3}) the parentheses will be too small:$(\frac{\sqrt x}{y^3})$.Using \left(... \right)' will make the sizes adjust automaticlly to the formula they enclose:\left(\frac{\sqrt x}{y^3}\right)` is $\left(\frac{\sqrt x}{y^3}\right)$.

    \left and \right apply to all the following sorts of parentheses: ( and ) $\left(x\right)$ ,[]{}|| like $\left[x\right] \left|x\right|$ , \langle and rangle $\langle x \rangle$ , \lceil x\rceil $\lceil x \rceil$,\lfloor \rfloor $\lfloor x \rfloor$ .There are alos invisible parentheses, denoted by .:\left.\frac12\right\rbrace is $\left.\frac12\right\rbrace$

  4. 求和和积分 \sum 和 \int；

    例子：

    \sum_1^n 显示 $\sum_1^n$

    \sum_{i=0}^\infty i^2 $\sum_{i=0}^\infty i^2$

    \prod \int \bigcup \bigcap \iint $\prod \int \bigcup \bigcap \iint$

  5. 分数

    有两种方式\frac ab $\frac ab$ 或者 \frac {...}{...} $\frac{2^x}{x+y}$ ; 当公式比较复杂的时候，可以考虑\over {2^x \over x+y} ${2^x \over x+y}$
