EF

Topic:Chatting about your job

Outcomes:

  • I can ask question about people’s jobs
  • I can give information about people’s jobs

    Useful expression

  • What do you do ?
  • How long have you been doing thar?
  • Do you enjoy your job ?
  • Do you work around here?
  • Where do you work?
  • At the moment,I’m working as a secretary
  • I usually enjoy it,but sometimes it’s a little stressful.
  • Just around the corner.
  • I work in a coffee shop

    WS：What do you do?- Talking about work

    Outcomes:

  • I can talk about how ofen I do something
  • I can tell someone what I do and introduce my job duties.

    Vocabulary

    Daily activities:make phone calls,talk to clients,do the schedule,write e-mails,go on business trip,do the figures,write reports,go to meetings
    Frequency:all the time,often/usually,sometimes,rerely,never
    Positions：manager,receptionlist,salesperson,secretary,accountant,assistant

    Useful expression:

  • What do you do every day?
  • What duties do you have in your job?
  • What do you do in your job?
