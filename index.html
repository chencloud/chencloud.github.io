MathJax basic tutorial and quick reference

  1. For inline formulas, enclose the formula in $...$. For displayed formulas, use $$...$$.
    These render differently: $\sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}$ (inline)or

    $$\sum_{i=0}^n i^{2} = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}\tag{display}$$

    the tex command:$$\sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}\tag{display}$$

  1. For Greek letters, use \alpha, \beta, …,\theta,\zeta,\pi,\omega: $\alpha$,$\beta$,…,$\theta$,$\zeta$,$\pi$,$\omega$. For uppercase, use \Gamma, \Delta, …, \Omega: $\Gamma$,$\Delta$,…,$\Omega$.

Topic9:Planing a trip

Preview

F2F:Planing a trip

Outcomes:

  • I can name holiday activities.
  • I can use ‘will’ and ‘can’ to talk about future

Vocabulary

  • go to a show
  • take a trip to the Great Wall
  • take some photos
  • buy some souvenirs
  • visit some museums
  • try the local food in a restaurant

    Useful expressions

  1. Which things do you like to do on holidays?
  2. How many days will they need? They’ll need …
  3. which places will you recommend to foreign vistors in China?

    I’m sure they will enjoy Beijing.
    They can go to a show, take a trip to the Great Wall, and take some photos at the Forbidden City

WS:Holidays and festivals-Deciding on an annual company event

Outcomes:

  • I can name the words related to a hotel,a festival or a campsite and events that a company can offer to its employee.
  • I can explain the reason why or why not I choose the event or place.

    Vocabulary

    Annual,event,activity,item,prefer
    Activities/events:do activities to build teamwork,hold meeting,hvae a message,have a special dinner,take part in races/contests,sing,campsite,hotel spa,music festival,city tour,solve prolblems,talk to others,visit gargens,see a show
    Things/items: you’ll need:money,laptop,a backpack,comfortable shoes,sunglasses,umbrella,sleeping bag,beach towel

    Useful expressions:

  • Which places would you prefer to go to ?

    I would prefer to go to …

  • Do we need to pay for that?
  • Dost it need money?

  • What do we need?

    1. We need comfortable shoes/swimsuit/unbrella/sumglasses/beach towel/backpack/sleeping bag/laptop…

    2. We don’t need a sleeping bag and beach towel
