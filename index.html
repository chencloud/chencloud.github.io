Topic02-Describing our daily routines

Preview

Outcomes:

I can use adverbs of frequency to describe daily routines.
我能使用频率副词
I can state own and different people's routines/habits using Present Simple.
我能对日常活动进行提问

重点句型

  1. What time do you…?你几点…（做什么)?
    What time do you get up?

  2. How ofen do you …?

    How ofen do you have parties?

  3. Do you like to …?

    Do you like to stay up late?

    词汇

  • Routimes

    get up;wash;go to work;eat;go home;watch TV;sleep

  • Cultural habits

    eat rice/bread/noodles for breakfast;have some tea/milk/coffee/wine/beer/coconut juice

  • Example

    Get up early,work 38 hours a week,and eat at 8 o’clock-10 o’clock in the evening, walk/travel to work)

    频率副词

    always occasionally

    usually rarely

    often never

    sometimes

时间顺序副词

开始故事 描述故事 结束故事
first next in the end
at the start then finally
after that

example
First,they meet a guy who they think is a nice guy.

Next,the father of one of the girls finds out what happened and tries to find her

Then,he goes to Pairs to investigate.

After that,he discovers who took her and why.

Finally,he finds her.

In the end,he rescues her, and it’s a happy ending.

  2. What did you think about

  3. I think that

    To be honest,
    In my opinion,
    Actually

表达意见时的重音和音调

  • 如果重读的单词是肯定的，则音调通常为升调
  • 如果重读的单词是否定的，则音调通常为降调
